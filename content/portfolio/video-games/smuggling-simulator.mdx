# Smuggling Simulator

A strategic simulation game where players manage complex wiring systems and smuggling operations through puzzle-solving.

Originally conceived as a wiring simulator, this game evolved into a unique smuggling simulation experience. Players must strategically plan routes, manage resources, and solve wiring puzzles to successfully complete smuggling operations, combining simulation and puzzle-solving elements for an engaging gameplay experience.
