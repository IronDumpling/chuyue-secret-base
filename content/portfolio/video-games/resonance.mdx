# Resonance

A 2D stealth platformer with innovative sound-based resonance mechanics for detecting enemies and navigating environments.

Developed in Unity, this unique platformer combines stealth gameplay with innovative resonance mechanics, where players use sound waves to detect enemies and navigate through challenging environments. The game features creative level design and requires strategic thinking to successfully complete each stage.
