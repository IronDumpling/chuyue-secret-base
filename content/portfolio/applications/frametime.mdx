# FrameTime

A time tracking and productivity management application with analytics and reporting features for task and project monitoring.

Developed as a collaborative project, FrameTime helps users efficiently track their time spent on different tasks and projects, providing insightful analytics and comprehensive reporting to improve productivity and time management.
